Hi, I'm a Principal Researcher at Huawei Canada and an Adjunct Professor at Queen's University.

At Huawei, I lead teams building LLM training and evaluation pipelines aimed at improving model capabilities in real-world software engineering tasks. My main research interests include SE4AI, AI4SE, and agentic software engineering. I am also interested in software ecosystems, software analytics, and programmable blockchains.

Before joining Queen's as an Adjunct Professor, I was a Research Fellow at the same university under the supervision of Prof. Ahmed Hassan. I earned my PhD from the University of São Paulo (USP) in Brazil under the supervision of Prof. Marco Gerosa. Before joining Huawei, I worked for IBM Brazil and Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE).
